The Platform

Explore surgical procedures using advanced interactive 3D tools. Engage with high-resolution videos and photorealistic anatomical models to navigate and understand complex surgical steps. Enhance your learning through detailed 3D reconstructions and assess your knowledge through integrated evaluation modules. We evaluated the effectiveness of this platform with dozens of medical students, residents, and senior surgeons. The results demonstrate that our new method significantly enhances the learning of surgical procedures compared to traditional approaches.

Reimagining Surgical Learning with Modern Tools

This study shows that an interactive 3D tool outperforms standard 2D videos in teaching total hip arthroplasty, especially for residents and users with prior 3D experience.

Video: Watch real surgical footage to understand each step.

Tools: Hover to highlight and explore surgical instruments in 3D.

Interactive Visualization: A 3D model with navigable and toggleable anatomical structures.

Evaluation: Test your knowledge through interactive assessments.
