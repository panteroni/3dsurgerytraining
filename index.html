The Platform

Explore surgical procedures using advanced interactive 3D tools designed to enhance surgical education. This platform has been evaluated with dozens of medical students, residents, and senior surgeons, showing significantly improved learning outcomes compared to traditional 2D video-based methods. By combining realistic visualization with interactive engagement, the platform helps users better understand complex surgical steps and develop spatial awareness critical to surgical practice. It serves as a foundation for a more immersive and effective approach to surgical training.

Reimagining Surgical Learning with Modern Tools

The platform allows users to study procedures—such as total hip arthroplasty—through a combination of high-resolution procedural videos and photorealistic 3D reconstructions. You can freely navigate anatomical models, explore labeled points of interest, and assess your knowledge through integrated evaluation modules. Available as a web demo or a full-featured downloadable version, the tool showcases the potential of interactive surgical education.

Video: Watch real surgical footage with a timeline highlighting key steps of the procedure.

Tools: Hover to highlight and explore surgical instruments in 3D.

Interactive Visualization: A 3D model with navigable and toggleable anatomical structures.

Evaluation: Test your knowledge through interactive assessments.
